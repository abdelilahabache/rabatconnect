Full description

Meet your guide at your hotel or riad in Rabat at 9:00 AM to begin your private half-day city tour. Travel comfortably in a private air-conditioned vehicle as you explore the city's most iconic historic sites.

Your tour includes visits to:

  • Mohamed V Avenue - Admire the blend of French colonial and traditional Moroccan architecture
  • Kasbah of the Udayas - Wander through blue-and-white streets of this 12th-century fortress
  • Hassan Tower - See the iconic unfinished minaret and learn its history
  • Royal Mausoleum - Pay respects at the resting place of King Mohammed V
  • Rabat Medina - Experience the sights, sounds, and smells of traditional Moroccan life

At the conclusion of your tour, enjoy lunch at a local restaurant before being returned to your accommodation or preferred location in Rabat.
